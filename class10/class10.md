> Q4:Water molecules normally have 3 atoms. Why do we see just one atom
> per water molecule in this structure?

Hydrogen atoms are usually not visible in X-ray structures because at
typical resolutions (~2 Å), their electron density is too weak to
detect, so only the oxygen atom of water is shown.
